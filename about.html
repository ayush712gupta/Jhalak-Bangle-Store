Our Story

Crafting Elegance Since 2013

Our Heritage

Founded in 2013, Jhalak Bangle Store has been a cornerstone of traditional and contemporary fashion accessories in India. What started as a small family shop has grown into a beloved destination for jewelry enthusiasts.

Our journey began in the bustling markets of Jaipur, where our founder, Mrs. Anjali Sharma, started crafting bangles using traditional techniques passed down through generations. Today, we continue to honor those traditions while embracing modern designs.
