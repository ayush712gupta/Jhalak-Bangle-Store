Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

We offer standard shipping which takes 3-5 business days and express shipping which takes 1-2 business days. International shipping may take 7-14 business days depending on the destination.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy. Items must be in original condition with all tags attached. Please contact us for a return authorization before sending items back.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes, we offer special wholesale pricing for retailers. Please contact us with your business details and requirements for our wholesale catalog and pricing.

How do I care for my bangles?

To maintain the shine and quality of your bangles, store them in a dry place away from moisture. Avoid contact with perfumes, lotions, and chemicals. Clean with a soft cloth after use.
